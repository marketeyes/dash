About Open Interest

Volume is the number of contracts traded in the current day, this includes both the buy and sell side of the trade. Therefore, a large amount of volume is indicitive of particular interest of a stock from a large fund or market player.

MarketEyes

MarketEyes is a financial data visualization tool that provides a comprehensive view of the stock market. It is designed to help traders and investors make informed decisions by providing real-time data on stock prices, volume, and options flows. MarketEyes uses advanced data analytics and visualization techniques to present complex financial data in an easy-to-understand format. The platform is designed to be user-friendly and intuitive, making it easy for traders and investors to access the information they need to make smart investment decisions.

Open Interest reflects the number of open contracts for a Stock at the end of each trading day. Values for OI are updated at 8:00 AM EST the following day, therefore the data is a lagging indicator of the previous day's activity.
